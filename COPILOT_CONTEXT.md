## Core Definition

```
GATAF (Governed AI Team Acceleration Framework) is an open governance framework
designed to help enterprise teams safely integrate AI into their daily workflows
by mapping existing work, aligning with corporate policy, and co-creating
guardrails that enable transparent human–AI collaboration.
```

**Primary outcome:** Human–AI–Team Synergy

Results superior to:
- humans alone
- AI alone
- unstructured collaboration

---

## Problem Being Solved

Enterprise AI adoption struggles because:

- Teams lack operational standards
- Governance is unclear
- AI usage transparency is inconsistent
- Policy alignment is difficult
- Collaboration patterns are undefined

GATAF introduces a lightweight governance framework that operates **inside existing infrastructure**. No new platform required.

---

## Immutable Framework Principles

These principles are **non-negotiable** and must not be modified unless explicitly directed by the project owner.

```
1. Corporate Policies as Foundation
   All standards must derive from corporate policy.

2. No Invalidation Rule
   Lower-level standards cannot override higher-level policy.

3. Framework Validation Mandatory
   Major outputs require policy validation.

4. Transparency
   AI contributions must be explainable in <30 seconds.

5. On-Demand Status Determinability
   Status of any work item must be instantly visible.

6. Inclusive Ideation
   Ideas may be captured via any natural medium.

7. Emergent Adaptation
   New collaboration patterns are expected and encouraged.

8. Synergy Pursuit
   Framework design should increase human-AI synergy.

9. Open Source Intent
   Framework will be released under Apache 2.0.
```

---

## Framework Architecture

The framework contains five core layers:

```
Layer 1 – Work Genome Mapping
Document current work activities, inputs, tools, and policies.

Layer 2 – Acceleration Opportunity Scan
Identify tasks that could benefit from AI assistance.

Layer 3 – Team Standards & Guardrails
Define acceptable AI behaviors and governance rules.

Layer 4 – Implementation Rituals
Daily/weekly/monthly operational collaboration patterns.

Layer 5 – Measurement & Feedback
Track transparency compliance, synergy outcomes, and adoption.
```
