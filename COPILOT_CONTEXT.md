## Core Definition

GATAF is an open governance framework designed to help enterprise teams safely integrate AI into their daily workflows by:

- Mapping existing work (inputs, outputs, tools, policies)
- Aligning with corporate policy at all levels
- Co-creating guardrails that enable transparent human–AI collaboration

**Primary outcome:** Human–AI–Team Synergy — results superior to humans alone, AI alone, or unstructured collaboration.

---

## Problem Being Solved

Enterprise AI adoption struggles because:

- Teams lack operational standards
- Governance is unclear
- AI usage transparency is inconsistent
- Policy alignment is difficult
- Collaboration patterns are undefined

GATAF introduces a lightweight governance framework that operates **inside existing infrastructure**. No new platform required.

---

## Immutable Framework Principles

> ⚠️ These principles are non-negotiable. Copilot must not suggest modifications to these principles unless explicitly directed by the project owner.

1. **Corporate Policies as Foundation** – All standards must derive from corporate policy.
2. **No Invalidation Rule** – Lower-level standards cannot override higher-level policy.
3. **Framework Validation Mandatory** – Major outputs require policy validation.
4. **Transparency** – AI contributions must be explainable in under 30 seconds.
5. **On-Demand Status Determinability** – Status of any work item must be instantly visible.
6. **Inclusive Ideation** – Ideas may be captured via any natural medium.
7. **Emergent Adaptation** – New collaboration patterns are expected and encouraged.
8. **Synergy Pursuit** – Framework design should increase human–AI synergy.
9. **Open Source Intent** – Framework will be released under Apache 2.0.

## Framework Architecture

### Five Core Layers

| Layer | Name | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Work Genome Mapping | Document current work activities, inputs, tools, and policies |
| 2 | Acceleration Opportunity Scan | Identify tasks that could benefit from AI assistance |
| 3 | Team Standards & Guardrails | Define acceptable AI behaviors and governance rules |
| 4 | Implementation Rituals | Daily/weekly/monthly operational collaboration patterns |
| 5 | Measurement & Feedback | Track transparency compliance, synergy outcomes, adoption |

### Three Supporting Engines

| Engine | Function |
|---|---|
| Ideation Intake Engine | Captures raw ideas from team members via any medium |
| Emergent Pattern Discovery Engine | Detects new human–AI collaboration patterns |
| Policy Validation Engine | Ensures compliance with corporate governance |
