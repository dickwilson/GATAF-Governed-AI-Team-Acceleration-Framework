## Instructions

Complete this template with your team during a dedicated Work Genome Mapping session. The goal is to create a shared, accurate understanding of what your team does — before determining where AI can help.

**Facilitation tip:** Don't over-engineer the first pass. Aim for ~80% accuracy. The Work Genome is a living document.
