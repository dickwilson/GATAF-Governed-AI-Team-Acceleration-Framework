# Team Work Genome Template

> **GATAF Layer 1 Artifact** | Work Genome Mapping
>
> Use this template to document your team's current work activities, inputs, tools, and policy constraints. The completed Work Genome becomes the foundation for all subsequent GATAF layers.

---

## Instructions

1. Complete one section per major work area or workflow.
2. Be descriptive — the more detail captured here, the better the acceleration scan will perform.
3. Involve the whole team. Different members have different views of the same work.
4. Review completed entries against applicable corporate policy before finalizing.
5. Validate the completed Work Genome using the [`validation-checklist.md`](validation-checklist.md).
