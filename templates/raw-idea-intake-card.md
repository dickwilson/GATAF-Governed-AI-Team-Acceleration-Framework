# Raw Idea Intake Card

> **GATAF Ideation Intake Engine Artifact**
>
> Use this card to capture any idea, observation, concern, or insight related to human–AI collaboration on your team. There is no wrong format. Submit as text, voice memo, sketch photo, or any other medium your team has enabled. The goal is to capture ideas before they are lost — refinement comes later.

## Instructions

- Fill in as much or as little as you know right now.
- Required fields are marked with *.
- If submitting via voice or sketch, a team facilitator will transcribe and complete the card.
- Submit to the team's Ideation Intake channel or owner.
