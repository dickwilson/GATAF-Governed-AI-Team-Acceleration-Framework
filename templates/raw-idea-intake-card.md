# Raw Idea Intake Card

**Governed AI Team Acceleration Framework – Ideation Intake Engine Artifact**
*Version: Pre-v1 | Author: Richard "Dick" Wilson (@idothuman)*

---

## Purpose

The Raw Idea Intake Card is designed for one thing: **capture the idea now, evaluate it later**.

Per Principle 6 (Inclusive Ideation), ideas may arrive in any form — a voice note, a chat message, a sketch, a hallway conversation. This card is the standardized intake form that converts any raw idea into a trackable work item.

**Key rule:** Do not filter or evaluate ideas at intake. Capture everything. Evaluation happens during the monthly Ideation Review.

---

## How to Submit an Idea

1. Fill out as much of this card as you can — even a partial card is valid
2. Submit via your team's designated intake channel (email, shared doc, chat bot, etc.)
3. The Framework Owner acknowledges receipt within 1 business day
4. The idea is added to the team's Idea Backlog
5. It will be reviewed at the next monthly Ideation Review session
