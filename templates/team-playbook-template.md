## Instructions

This template guides teams through creating their GATAF Team Playbook — the living document that governs how their team collaborates with AI. It combines the outputs of Layers 1–4.

A completed playbook should be:
- **Grounded** in corporate policy (not invented in a vacuum)
- **Agreed** upon by the full team
- **Validated** through the Policy Validation Engine
- **Accessible** to any team member at any time
- **Living** — updated as standards evolve and new patterns emerge

## Section 6: AI Disclosure Protocol

All AI-assisted outputs produced by this team must follow the disclosure protocol below.

### 6.1 Disclosure Requirement Levels

| Level | Applies When | Required Disclosure |
|---|---|---|
| **Level 1 – Minor** | AI used for grammar, formatting, or spell check only | Optional brief note |
| **Level 2 – Moderate** | AI drafted sections, generated options, or analyzed data | Standard disclosure label required |
| **Level 3 – Significant** | AI generated primary content, recommendations, or analysis | Full disclosure block required |
| **Level 4 – Major** | AI produced the substantive work product | Full disclosure + Policy Validation required |

### 6.2 Standard Disclosure Label (Level 2)

```
AI-Assisted Content
Tool: [AI tool name]
Role: [Brief description, e.g., "drafted initial outline"]
Human Review: [Reviewer name] on [date]
```

### 6.3 Full Disclosure Block (Levels 3 & 4)

```
AI Contribution Disclosure
──────────────────────────
Tool(s) Used: [AI tool name(s)]
Date of AI Assistance: [date]
AI Contribution: [Describe what AI did — explainable in ≤ 30 seconds]
Human Review: [Reviewer name], [role], on [date]
Edits Made by Human: [Yes / No / Describe]
Policy Validation: [Validated / Pending]
Accountable Owner: [Human name and role]
```
