## Part 4: AI Disclosure Protocol *(required)*

> *Define how the team discloses AI contributions in its work products.*

### 4.1 Disclosure Standard

All team outputs that involved AI assistance must include a disclosure that meets the GATAF 30-Second Transparency Standard: any authorized stakeholder must be able to understand the nature of AI's contribution within 30 seconds of asking.

### 4.2 Disclosure Method

> *How does your team disclose AI contributions? Describe the mechanism(s) used.*

☐ **Disclosure footer** – Standard disclosure footer appended to AI-assisted outputs
