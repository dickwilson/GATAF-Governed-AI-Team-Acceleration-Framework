# Policy Validation Checklist

**Governed AI Team Acceleration Framework – Policy Validation Engine Artifact**
*Version: Pre-v1 | Author: Richard "Dick" Wilson (@idothuman)*

---

## Purpose

This checklist is the operational tool for the **Policy Validation Engine** — the GATAF supporting engine that ensures all team-level AI standards and major outputs comply with corporate governance.

Per Principle 3 (Framework Validation Mandatory), any major output produced with AI assistance must be validated before use, distribution, or publication.

Per Principle 2 (No Invalidation Rule), no GATAF standard may override or weaken higher-level corporate policy.

---

## When to Use This Checklist

Use for **either** of the following:

1. **Validating a new GATAF standard or guardrail** (before adding to the Team Playbook)
2. **Validating a major AI-assisted output** (before distribution or publication)
