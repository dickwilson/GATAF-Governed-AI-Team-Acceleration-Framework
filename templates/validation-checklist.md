# Policy Validation Checklist

> **GATAF Policy Validation Engine Artifact**
>
> Use this checklist to validate any major GATAF artifact against applicable corporate policy before adoption. Policy validation is mandatory per **Principle 3: Framework Validation Mandatory**. Complete this checklist for Team Playbooks, Work Genomes, Standards documents, and any other significant framework output.

---

## Instructions

1. Identify the artifact being validated and the policies that apply to it.
2. Work through each checklist section with the relevant policy documents accessible.
3. Note any conflicts, ambiguities, or items requiring legal or compliance review.
4. Obtain required sign-offs before the artifact is formally adopted.
5. Retain this completed checklist with the validated artifact.
