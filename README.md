# GATAF – Governed AI Team Acceleration Framework

> **An open governance framework for safe, transparent, and measurable human–AI team collaboration.**

## What Is GATAF?

**GATAF (Governed AI Team Acceleration Framework)** is an open governance framework designed to help enterprise teams safely integrate AI into their daily workflows by:

- Mapping existing work activities, inputs, and tools
- Aligning AI adoption with corporate policy
- Co-creating guardrails that enable transparent human–AI collaboration

The primary outcome GATAF pursues is **Human–AI–Team Synergy** — results that are superior to what humans alone, AI alone, or unstructured collaboration could produce.

---

## The Problem GATAF Solves

Enterprise AI adoption struggles because:

- Teams lack operational standards for working with AI
- Governance structures are unclear or nonexistent
- AI usage transparency is inconsistent
- Policy alignment is difficult to maintain
- Human–AI collaboration patterns are undefined

GATAF introduces a lightweight governance framework that operates **inside existing infrastructure**. No new platform is required.

---

## Framework Architecture

GATAF is organized into five core layers:

| Layer | Name | Purpose |
|-------|------|---------|
| 1 | Work Genome Mapping | Document current work activities, inputs, tools, and policies |
| 2 | Acceleration Opportunity Scan | Identify tasks that could benefit from AI assistance |
| 3 | Team Standards & Guardrails | Define acceptable AI behaviors and governance rules |
| 4 | Implementation Rituals | Daily/weekly/monthly operational collaboration patterns |
| 5 | Measurement & Feedback | Track transparency compliance, synergy outcomes, and adoption |

Three **Supporting Engines** operate across all layers:

- **Ideation Intake Engine** – Captures raw ideas from team members
- **Emergent Pattern Discovery Engine** – Detects new human–AI collaboration patterns
- **Policy Validation Engine** – Ensures compliance with corporate governance

See [`/docs/gataf-framework-architecture-v1.png`](docs/gataf-framework-architecture-v1.png) for the visual architecture diagram.

## Core Principles

GATAF is guided by nine immutable principles:

1. **Corporate Policies as Foundation** – All standards must derive from corporate policy
2. **No Invalidation Rule** – Lower-level standards cannot override higher-level policy
3. **Framework Validation Mandatory** – Major outputs require policy validation
4. **Transparency** – AI contributions must be explainable in under 30 seconds
5. **On-Demand Status Determinability** – Status of any work item must be instantly visible
6. **Inclusive Ideation** – Ideas may be captured via any natural medium
7. **Emergent Adaptation** – New collaboration patterns are expected and encouraged
8. **Synergy Pursuit** – Framework design should increase human–AI synergy
9. **Open Source Intent** – Framework released under Apache 2.0

See [`/framework/principles.md`](framework/principles.md) for full detail.
