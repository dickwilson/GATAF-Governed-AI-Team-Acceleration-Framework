## Nature of the Invention / Concept

GATAF is an original governance framework concept for the structured, transparent, and policy-aligned integration of AI into enterprise team workflows. It is not a software product, platform, or algorithm. It is a **meta-framework** — a set of principles, layers, templates, rituals, and measurement approaches — designed to be adopted inside any existing organizational infrastructure without requiring new tooling.

The core intellectual contribution is the following combination of ideas:

1. **Work Genome Mapping** — treating a team's current work activities as structured, mappable data that can be analyzed for AI acceleration opportunities.
2. **Layered Governance Architecture** — a five-layer model that moves from discovery through implementation to feedback in a repeatable cycle.
3. **Immutable Principles** — a set of non-negotiable governance rules that anchor all team-level standards to corporate policy, preventing governance drift.
4. **Human–AI–Team Synergy** as a primary measurable outcome — explicitly targeting results superior to humans alone, AI alone, or ad-hoc collaboration.
5. **Supporting Engines** — three cross-cutting systems (Ideation Intake, Emergent Pattern Discovery, Policy Validation) that operate across all layers.
6. **Transparent AI Disclosure Protocol** — requiring all AI contributions to be explainable within 30 seconds by any team member.

- Work genome mapping
- Emergent pattern discovery
- Synergy measurement

…into a single lightweight, platform-independent team acceleration model at the date of this disclosure.
